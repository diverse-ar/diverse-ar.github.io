Abstract

In this paper, we investigate the underexplored challenge of sample diversity in autoregressive (AR) generative models with bitwise visual tokenizers. We initially analyze the factors limiting diversity in bitwise AR models and identify two key issues: (1) the binary classification nature of bitwise modeling, and (2) the overly-sharp logits distribution which causes sampling collapse...
